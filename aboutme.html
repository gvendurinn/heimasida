About me

Born 1999 in Reykjavík, Iceland. Studying Mechanical Engineering at University of Iceland. Currently in a post-punk band called Kisimja. Interests include music, problem solving and design/fabrication.

Previous jobs have been in retail, construction and automotive electrics and repair field for example. I have worked on many automotive restorations and currently building a heavily modified Suzuki Samurai. One day I hope to open a fabrication shop.
